Lasagna Recipe

How to Make Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef (or pork, or a mix)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 700g jar of marinara sauce (or homemade tomato sauce)
  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat and sauté onion and garlic until soft. Add the ground beef, season with salt and pepper, cook until browned, then stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, a little salt, pepper, and Parmesan to make the cheese filling.
  4. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, add a layer of noodles, then spread some ricotta mixture and sprinkle mozzarella. Repeat the layers until you finish the ingredients, ending with sauce and mozzarella on top, then sprinkle Parmesan.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake uncovered for another 15-20 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  6. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with fresh basil if you like.
