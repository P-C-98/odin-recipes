Butternut Squash Risotto

How to Make Butternut Squash Risotto

This butternut squash risotto takes a little time to prepare, but the process is simple. Heres how it goes:

Ingredients

  • 1 small butternut squash, peeled and diced into small cubes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 300g arborio rice
  • 1 liter vegetable or chicken stock (keep warm)
  • 100ml dry white wine (optional)
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh sage or parsley (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F). Toss butternut squash cubes in olive oil, season with salt and pepper, then roast for 20-25 minutes until tender and caramelized.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan and cook onion and garlic until soft, then stir in arborio rice and cook for 1-2 minutes to coat the grains.
  3. If using, add the wine and stir until it is fully absorbed.
  4. Gradually add warm stock, one ladle at a time, stirring often and allowing each addition to absorb before adding the next. Continue for 20-25 minutes until the rice is creamy and al dente.
  5. Stir in the roasted squash, butter, Parmesan, and season to taste.
  6. Let the risotto sit for 2 minutes, then serve with extra Parmesan and fresh herbs if desired.
