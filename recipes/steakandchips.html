Steak and Chips

How to Make Butternut Squash Risotto

This is a quick and easy recipe to make a delicious steak and chips.

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye or sirloin steaks
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into chips
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for steak)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • Fresh thyme or rosemary (optional)

Instructions

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thick chips, then soak them in cold water for 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  2. Drain and dry the chips, then fry them in hot vegetable oil (about 160째C / 320째F) for 4-5 minutes until soft but not golden. Remove and set aside.
  3. Season the steaks generously with salt and pepper. Heat a pan with olive oil over high heat and sear the steaks for 2-3 minutes per side for medium-rare, adjusting time for desired doneness.
  4. In the last minute of cooking, add butter, crushed garlic, and fresh herbs to the pan, basting the steaks with the melted butter for extra flavor.
  5. Rest the steaks for 5 minutes while you increase the oil temperature to 190째C (375째F) and fry the chips again for 2-3 minutes until golden and crispy.
  6. Serve the steaks with the crispy chips and your choice of sauce or side salad.
